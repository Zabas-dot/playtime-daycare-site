Our Philosophy

At Playtime Daycare Centre, we provide a warm, family-centered environment where children feel safe, happy, and excited to learn every day.

设

A True Partnership

We work closely with our families to build a strong, supportive community. When parents and educators work together, children feel a deeper sense of security and belonging.

⭐

Learning Through Play

Children are naturally curious and capable. We structure our days around their unique interests, encouraging them to explore, ask questions, and discover the world at their own pace.

📖

Ontario Early Years Guidelines

Our programs are built on the foundations of Ontario's "How Does Learning Happen?" framework, focusing on your child's well-being, engagement, expression, and belonging.

☀️

Well-Rounded Growth

From outdoor playtime and nature walks to sensory crafts and quiet reading circles, we make sure every day is balanced with active movement, creative expression, and healthy rest.

Enrollment & Experiences

How Enrollment Works

1

Join the City of Ottawa Waitlist

Families must register through the City of Ottawa childcare waitlist system.

2

Select Playtime Daycare Centre

Add our centre as one of your preferred childcare options.

3

We Review Your Profile

When a space becomes available, we review your placement on the waitlist.

4

Tour Invitation

If selected, we contact you directly to schedule a tour and next steps.
